Style Guide & Tips

The Rule of Three

A great outfit typically uses no more than three main colors. This keeps the look cohesive and prevents visual clutter.

Understand Undertones

Match cool colors (blues, greens) with silver accessories, and warm colors (reds, browns) with gold to enhance your natural complexion.

Neutral Foundation

Build your wardrobe around neutrals like Navy, Black, and Beige. They serve as the perfect base for any outfit.

About the Project
